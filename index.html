Services

ENDOSCOPY/LAPAROSCOPIC SURGERIES

We are equipped to avail our numerous patients the alternative as well as the advantage they need in imaging diagnostics. We run a full upper and lower GI endoscopies: these include Oesophagoscopy, Gastroscopy, Duodenoscopy and Colonoscopies.

GENERAL CONSULTATION

24 hours a day, 7 days a week, our GPs are consulting. Seasoned practitioners, they are always readily on hand for routine consultations and management of medical emergencies.

ANTE-NATAL/ MATERNITY

Some people call us BABY CATCHERS. Over the years, since delivering our first baby DAVID NNAJI, on 18th June, 1996, we have built a reputation for excellent maternity services. We run a rich ante-natal programme for our mothers. The appointments and sessions usually involve teachings, interactions, routine examinations and investigations. Mothers are guided through this period by very experienced gynaecologists and mid-wives.

RADIO IMAGING

The world is getting smaller in functional size as made possible by technology. The imaging and radiological department provide very essential services critical to the overall care of the patient. The medical world has moved from printing and carrying large size X-Ray films to weightless, intangible soft copies transmitted online, real time. We have met this standard with our digital radiological facilities. Here, X-Ray images are automatically lunched into the email address or Whatsapp page of the patient and or his or her doctor in the shortest time imaginable.

LABORATORY SERVICES

This is the cornerstone of diagnostic and therapeutic medicine. Patient care is not complete, not effective, is hardly sufficient without it. Most of the physician's diagnosis remain tentative until a laboratory or imaging investigation provides evidence based confirmation.

INTENSIVE CARE UNIT

There comes a time in the disease progression of some patients or an acute state when respiration fails and disease prognosis shrinks. At such moments, patient's life is severely threatened if something is not done urgently. In such situations therefore, every minute counts. The hospital has put in place a LIFE SUPPORT UNIT to come to the patient's rescue at such a time as described.

DIALYSIS

We run a nephrology clinic. To complement this unit at a time when statistics show a rising incidence of renal impairment, we have established a dialysis unit to help the numerous patients whose clinical conditions daily demand this service. With a dedicated team attached to the unit, it is available and accessible 24/7 and it has restored hope and indeed, life to many.

MEDICAL CHECK-UPS

This is our concept of preventive care and the principle of early detection and early intervention for healthy living and better treatment outcomes. We have a wellness program that encourages apparently healthy people to consult the doctor and run relevant laboratory and imaging investigations for regular, routine health checks at discounted cost. This is the Meridian Life Buoy. It can be done at four categories of BRONZE, SILVER, GOLD and PLATINUM, as will be determined by the person's needs and circumstances.

WARD EXPERIENCE

We make the patient's hospital stay friendly, comfortable and memorable. The wards are roomy, clean, with a homely furnishing and patients receive such attention that makes them forget they are sick. We have accommodation categorized into: General wards for 3 or 4, Semi-Private wards for 2, Amenity (Private) wards for 1 and Suites for more than just ordinary private ward experience. Some suites have kitchenette for full privacy and personalization of the patient's stay

BABY UNIT

The duration of pregnancy in man is 38 to 42 weeks. The doctor is happy to deliver the pregnant mother within this period and certainly, babies are happy to come at this time. This is when we say the baby is mature and able to live outside the womb successfully. These babies are nursed under such special conditions as to offer them a better chance to life.

FAMILY PLANNING

A planned family, planned parent hood is the cornerstone of family health and by extension, family economy. Couples are guided to make informed choice of their family planning option. We provide very useful guide to our clients on the Billing's (Natural) method of family planning, intra uterine contraceptive devices (IUCDs), Oral pills, injectable alternatives, in-plants, surgical contraceptives; Bilateral Tubal Ligation (BTL) and Vasectomies.

INTERNATIONAL REFERRALS

Our commitment to delivering world-class healthcare extends beyond borders, and we understand that seeking medical expertise sometimes requires crossing international boundaries. Our International Referral Program is designed to provide seamless access to our cutting-edge medical services, ensuring that patients worldwide receive the exceptional care they deserve.

HOME CARE SERVICES

Some patients' condition may not permit them the convenience and ease of travelling to the hospital and may at the same time not warrant continuous hospital stay for economic, social and other reasons. We provide home care services for such patients. Other career persons may wish to be consulted in the office for the convenience and office efficiency it affords them. Again, we meet such needs.

KITCHEN SERVICES

At Meridian hospital, we recognize the integral role nutrition plays in the healing process. Our dedicated kitchen services go beyond traditional expectations, aiming to provide nourishing and well-balanced meals tailored to support the health and recovery of our patients. With a focus on culinary expertise, safety, and dietary considerations, our kitchen services are designed to enhance the overall well-being of those under our care.

AMBULANCE SERVICES

A set of ambulances fully equipped with modern life saving gadgets such as Automatic External Defibrillator (AED), Nebulizer, Ventilator, Oxygen source, Suction Machine, etc. coordinate our Emergency Medical Services. This is supported by well trained and very competent personnel on the ready, 24/7. Ambulance services are available 24/7. Through our dedicated telephone lines, the SOS lines, response is readily triggered by a call away.

Cardiology

A comprehensive cardiology clinic runs here as an exclusively consultant clinic. Cardiovascular diseases, including hypertension and cardiovascular accidents (CVA) and CVA related mortalities are on the increase in the society. The unit has facility for regular resting ECG as well as an auto assessed, advanced Stress ECG to complement. In addition, Echocardiography facility is available in-house and patients are availed its services as may be necessary for better outcomes.

Physiotherapy

We treat injuries, diseases and disorders through physical methods — such as exercise, massage, manipulation and other treatments — over medication and surgery. Many people may be of the opinion that physiotherapists mainly work with back and sports related injuries, but they would be very mistaken. As Physiotherapists, we are highly trained health professionals who provide treatment for people suffering from physical problems arising from injury, disease, illness and ageing. Our aim is to improve a person's quality of life by using a variety of treatments to alleviate pain and restore function or, in the case of permanent injury or disease, to lessen the effects of any dysfunction.

Obstetrics and Gynaecology

Obstetrics and gynaecology are twin sisters and while obstetrics deals with pregnancy and childbirth, gynaecology contemplates and addresses the conditions affecting the reproductive system of women. One naturally connects to the other. These constitute a major dimension of our practice. Over the years, our obstetricians and midwives have progressively transformed and redefined this arm of patient experience.

General Practice

Our general practitioners are available to manage all kinds of illness presented in an undifferentiated way at an early stage of development, which may require urgent intervention. Our clinic holistic approach of general practice aims to take into consideration the biological, psychological, and social factors relevant to the care of each patient's illness.
